Our Mission

The mission of Brain Stew is to promote socio, economic, health, education and culture through participatory approach focusing children, adolescents and women, who are being deprived and oppressed in the society.

Our Vision

The vision of Brain Stew is to mitigate the sufferings, turmoil, trails & tribulations of deprived and downtrodden sections of the society to ensure equity, empowerment and justice.

About Brain Stew

Welcome to our foundation

Brain Stew is a non-government organization ngo, working as a charitable society or trust for the welfare and development of deprived street children, orphans, destitute women, poor old age people.

  • Brain Stew has been working its need-based programs focused on access to education, food, livelihood training and healthcare.
  • Spreading awareness among the public on health & sanitation, education, environment, consumer rights, road safety and other socio-economic rights particularly Human rights.

Brain Stew Orphanage

Charitable Old Age Home

At Brain Stew Home for the Aged, we have adopted good practices in order to provide a healthy, productive and participatory life to the elderly women residents. We are able to run this old age home with generous contributions from individuals like you, and corporates.
